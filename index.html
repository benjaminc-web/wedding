Where

Płock, located in central Poland along the Vistula River, is a historic city known for its rich cultural heritage, medieval architecture, and vibrant atmosphere.

Both the cermony and reception will be at Goślice 15, 09-230 Goślice.

When

The wedding is set for Saturday, 3rd August 2024, promising a day filled with joy and celebration.

Wear

Prepare to dazzle in your finest suits and dresses as we come together to celebrate this joyous occasion.

It's a day to dress to impress and create beautiful memories that will last a lifetime.

Transport!

By plane

The best airport to get to Płock, Poland, is Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW). It is the largest international airport in the vicinity and is approximately 120 kilometers south-east of Płock.

From Warsaw Chopin Airport, you can easily reach Płock by various means of transportation, such as car rentals, taxis, or public transportation options like trains and buses.

By car

If you prefer the flexibility of traveling by car, reaching Płock is convenient as it is well-connected by roadways. Whether you're driving from nearby cities or starting your journey from Warsaw, navigating to Płock by car offers a scenic and comfortable travel experience.
