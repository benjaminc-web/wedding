MARTYNA & BEN

AUGUST 3RD 2024 • POLAND

Our story!

Martyna

After a last-minute decision to study abroad, Martyna packed her bags, music books and flute and spent a year in Alicante, Spain. It was there where her love for music, Spanish culture and the beach combined and where she met Ben in the kitchen of their shared apartment!

Ben

In 2020, Ben moved to Poland where he embraced the Polish culture and started learning the language. After three years, he decided to propose to Martyna on a very cold January day at a local park!

Details!

Where

The ceremony will take place at 1700 at Kolegialna 9, 09-402 Płock at the registry office. This will be followed by the reception at Różany Dworek Wedding House, Wola Łącka 10, 09-520 Wola Łącka.

Płock, located in central Poland along the Vistula River, is a historic city known for its rich cultural heritage, medieval architecture, and vibrant atmosphere.

When

The wedding is set for Saturday, 3rd August 2024, promising a day filled with joy and celebration.

Wear

Please wear formal attire. Prepare to dazzle in your finest suits and dresses as we come together to celebrate this joyous occasion.

It's a day to dress to impress and create beautiful memories that will last a lifetime.

Transport!

By plane

The best airports to get to Płock are either Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW) or Warsaw Modlin (WMI). From Warsaw, you can get a direct train to Kutno and change to Płock.

For guests travelling from England here are some options on Friday and Saturday to both of Warsaw's airports.

By car

If you prefer the flexibility of traveling by car, reaching Płock is convenient as it is well-connected by roadways.

From Warsaw to Płock it takes roughly 1.5 to 2 hours by car.
